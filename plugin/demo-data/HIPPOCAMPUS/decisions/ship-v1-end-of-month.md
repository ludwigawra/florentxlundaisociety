# Ship v1 end of month, not end of quarter

## Claim
We commit to shipping v1 by 2026-04-30. A smaller scope that actually lands, not a bigger scope that slips another quarter.

## Context
Debated between "ship the minimum viable core" (current scope) and "ship the full vision." Investor meeting on 2026-04-24 makes shipped-and-real more valuable than spec'd-and-impressive.

## Alternatives considered
- Ship in July with the full feature set. Rejected — we've slipped this date twice; a third time damages trust with the current backers.
- Ship a prototype for the investor meeting only. Rejected — prototypes read as prototypes. A real customer-facing v1 tells a different story.

## Rationale
Velocity compounds. One real v1 in the world generates more useful feedback in two weeks than three more months of speccing.
