# Hire a senior engineer, not a founding engineer, for role #4

## Claim
Role #4 is posted as "Senior Engineer" with no equity-heavy founding track. Standard comp, standard vesting.

## Context
Two candidates in flight: Maya (senior, wants stability) and one other (junior-ish, open to founding role). The founding engineer framing was attracting the wrong signal — people looking for the startup lottery rather than people who would ship.

## Alternatives considered
- Keep posting as founding engineer and wait longer. Rejected — we need someone shipping in 3 weeks, not 3 months.

## Rationale
The work right now is "make v1 land cleanly." That's senior eng work, not founding eng work. Reserve the founding title for someone we can't imagine shipping without.
