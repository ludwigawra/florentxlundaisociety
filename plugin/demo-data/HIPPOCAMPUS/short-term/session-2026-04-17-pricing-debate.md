# Pricing debate — session notes

Long debate on usage vs seat-based pricing. Key points:

- The strongest argument against usage-based was "unpredictable costs scare off the enterprise buyer." Counter: we are not selling to enterprise for v1.
- The strongest argument for seat-based was "predictable revenue for us." Counter: predictable revenue from the wrong customer profile is not an asset.
- Decision to go usage-based for v1. Revisit if/when enterprise ICP becomes real.
